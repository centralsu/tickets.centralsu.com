About the waiting list

What is the list for?

If you don’t manage to get a ticket, joining the waiting list means we’ll know to come to you if tickets become available. This could happen if someone returns their ticket to us, we revoke a ticket or we’re allocated more.

How will you contact me?

If we have a ticket available, we’ll send an email to your Central email account. You’ll have a limited time to get back to us before we move on to someone else on the list.

Can I have my friend’s ticket?

No, you can’t transfer tickets. If your friend doesn’t want theirs, they’ll need to contact us to get a refund. We’ll then offer the ticket to someone on the waiting list.
