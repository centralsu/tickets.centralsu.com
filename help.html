When is the party?

We’ll be starting from 8pm on Saturday 30 October and running until late.

Who will be there?

The event is open to all students at an alcopa college. That means students from Central, Guildhall, Royal Academy of Music, Royal College of Music and Trinity Laban.

Will I need to bring ID?

Yes, you’ll need proof of age such as a driving licence or passport at the door. You’ll also need your Central campus card to be allowed entry.

How do I find the bar?

A map probably. The address is:
Sundial Court
38–42 Chiswell Street
London  EC1Y 4SB

Is the venue accessible?

Yes, the bar is wheelchair accessible. It‘d be helpful if you could let us know when buying your ticket if you‘ll need to use the lift.

Will there be a cloak room?

Yes, the cloak room is straight ahead as you enter the Basement, signed ‘meeting room’. You can’t take alcohol in here and all items are left at your own risk

How do I get home afterwards?

We recommend downloading the free Citymapper app to help you plan your way around London. Last trains are usually just after midnight, but many bus services run 24 hours a day. Try not to travel alone late at night and speak to a member of staff if you need help or find yourself on your own.

Uber, Bolt and Free Now are examples of minicab booking apps. They are often much cheaper than black cabs, especially if you split the fare with friends.

Never use an unbooked minicab – they are dangerous and illegal.

What if I need help?

A few of our officers will be available, wearing a uniform to be visible. They’re happy to help if anything goes wrong. The bar staff and trained security are all there to make sure you have a good night. In an emergency, dial 999.

There will also be a dedicated space available for people to take a break from the club environment, signed ‘sundial court teaching one’. SU, security and bar staff will check this area regularly.

How will you keep me safe from coronavirus?

The Basement Bar is a club environment, so you should expect to be in close proximity with many people. In line with current government guidelines, it is not mandatory to wear a mask, but we ask you to be considerate in smaller enclosed areas such as the toilets. Many people will still feel more comfortable wearing a mask, and this should be welcomed and respected. We will provide hand sanitiser around the venue and we strongly recommend everyone coming takes a lateral flow test beforehand.

Do I need a ticket?

Yes, tickets are essential. The bar’s premises licence sets a capacity for the the number of people it can host safely and we can’t break this limit.

Can I get a ticket for my friend?

No, this event is only for students at an alcopa college. If your friend goes to Guildhall, RAM, RCM or Trinity Laban, they can buy a ticket from their students’ union.

Are there tickets on the door?

No, you need to buy a ticket in advance. There are only a very limited number of tickets available to each college and we expect to sell out quickly.

Am I guaranteed entry?

Your ticket gives you entry, subject to the rules. You’ll be turned away if you try to bring drugs in to the venue and might be removed if you’re acting in a dangerous way or causing a disturbance.

How will I know you’ve got my order?

Once you have placed your order successfully we will take you to a page confirming that we’ve got your order and you’re on the list. PayPal will also send you a confirmation email with your unique transaction ID.

If you don’t see this page or receive an email, contact us.

Can I give my ticket to someone else?

No, you can’t transfer your ticket to someone else. If you don’t want it, you’ll need to contact us to get a refund. We’ll then offer your ticket to someone on the waiting list.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, you can contact us to request a refund up until 10am on the day of the event. We’ll offer your ticket to someone on the waiting list.

What if the party is cancelled?

We hope it doesn’t come to it, but will let you know as soon as possible if we need to cancel the party. We’ll then do everything we can to recover our costs and pass these savings back on to you.
